Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Rock Valley Chamber of Commerce serves as an economic hub that provides a community of bussiness minded people, entreprenuer networks and avenue for investors to find and patner with promising business and individuals to bring about economic growth for the Rock Valley municipality. This is the place where the economic developments and policies are created and set rolling for the prosperity of businesses and our community.

Scenarios

Q1: What business group are likely to thrive by patnering with you?
Almost every bussiness enterprise will greatly benefit from the policies and networking resources we provide. There seasoned members and networks for many business fields including agribusiness, processing, retails, textiles, technology and more. There are experts who are invited on occasions to provide insight and additional external resources that may benefit our members.

Q2: When are your meeting times and do offer online avenue to participate in meetings?
We hold a bi-weekly meeting for our members based on the area of business interest and specializations. We also hold a monthly meeting for all members to discuss policies and how it is affecting our business and the community at large. Members have the avenue to join live meetings online and participate in discussion through an online media and chatroom we provide.

Q3:What are some of the events you hold to promote the growth of start-up business?
With the help of our seasoned entreprenuerial members, we offer coaching and mentorship to young entreprenuers. We orginize a quaterly "Novice time with Seasoned Entreprenuers" event where new business owners get the opportunity to learn from the seasoned and successful business owners for coaching mentorship avenues. A time to make connections with establish businesses who can promote or patronize your services.
